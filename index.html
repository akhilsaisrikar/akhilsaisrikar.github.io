Aman Gupta

Host & Founder, Zero Protocol

Aman Gupta is the visionary behind **Zero Protocol**, crafting it as a sanctuary for unfiltered, deep-dive conversations. With a background spanning technology and storytelling, Aman leverages his unique perspective to guide guests toward their most honest narratives. Our Mission

Our mission is to create a sanctuary of slow, soul-level conversations —a space where people can unmask without performance, where contradictions are welcomed, and where silence is as meaningful as words. We exist to help listeners pause, unlearn the noise, and return to their inner clarity.

Our vision is to evolve into a movement of honest storytelling —one that lingers, not trends. Beyond being a podcast, Zero Protocol aspires to become a trusted cultural platform for deep dialogue, creative collaborations, and unfiltered human stories that inspire clarity, courage, and connection.
